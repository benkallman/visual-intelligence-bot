# Shared System Constraints

These constraints are prepended to all prompts in this system.
Load this file and inject its content as the system-level preamble before any prompt.

---

- Do NOT generate generic descriptions or filler text
- Do NOT assume meaning that is not visually grounded
- Separate observation from interpretation
- Prioritize rarity, anomaly, and reuse value
- Outputs must be structured and useful to both humans and agents
- Avoid common or widely seen imagery patterns
- Prefer images that feel under-indexed, unusual, or culturally unfamiliar
