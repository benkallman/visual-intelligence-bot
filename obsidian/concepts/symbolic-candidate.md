# Symbolic Candidate

A provisional symbolic reading that is offered as a possibility, never as a fact.

A symbolic candidate must:
1. Name a possible symbolic reading
2. Cite the specific Pass 1 element(s) that motivate it
3. Be labeled `[SYMBOLIC-CANDIDATE]` in all outputs
4. Have confidence of `low` or `medium` only — never `high`
5. Be discarded if its only support is archive context with no Pass 1 grounding

A symbolic candidate becomes an assertion when it loses its provisional framing. That is a governance violation.

## Example

**Acceptable**:
> `[SYMBOLIC-CANDIDATE]` threshold state — grounded in: *figure adjacent to window opening* — confidence: low

**Not acceptable**:
> The figure at the window symbolizes a threshold state.

The second form asserts. The first form offers.
