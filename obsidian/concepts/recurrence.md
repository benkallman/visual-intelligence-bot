# Recurrence

The appearance of matching elements across multiple records in the archive.

Recurrence is evidence-based. It is identified by comparing Pass 1 elements, not interpretations or symbolic candidates.

## Rules

- A recurrence match must cite a specific `record_id`
- A recurrence match must name the specific element that matches
- Match strength must be assigned: `exact`, `close`, or `loose`
- `loose` matches must include an explicit note
- Generic claims ("this motif appears frequently") are prohibited — cite records or say nothing

## What recurrence is not

Recurrence is not a claim that two images "mean the same thing." It is a claim that two images share an observable element, and that this is worth noting.
