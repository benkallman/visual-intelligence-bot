# Witness

The stance of direct observation without interpretation.

A witness records what is present. A witness does not explain, symbolize, or narrate.

In the context of this system, Pass 1 operates in witness mode. Every element listed in Pass 1 must be something a witness could state without inference.

**Test**: Could a person describe this element in a photograph deposition without being challenged for speculation? If yes, it belongs in Pass 1.
